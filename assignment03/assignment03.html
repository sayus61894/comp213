The PlayStation 4

The Playstation is one of the most popular gaming consoles to come in most recent years.
Supporting some of the best graphics and gaming experiences it has sold more than 100 millions as of Oct 2019.
Has a strong library of games that it shares with other consoles including:

  • Call of Duty
  • Borderlands
  • Fortnite

Also has an incredible library of exclusives games only available on PS4:

  • Persona 5
  • God of War
  • Uncharted
  • Marvel's Spiderman

Below are the PS4 models that are currently on the market. Due to the updates in the hardware, some are easier to find compared to the others.
Clicking the images will enlarge them.

 

 
Playstation 4

  • Released in 2013, this is the original PS4 model. This is harder to find compared to current models. Supports hi-def graphics.
  • Exclusive PS4 Games
  • 500 GB replacable hard Drive
  • Comes in Black or White
 

 
Playstation 4 Slim

  • Released in 2016, this is a slimmer model of the orignal PS4. Due to the slimness of the model, it can fit in just about any place.
  • Sports the same graphics quality as the original model.
  • Whisper Quiet
  • 500 GB replacable hard Drive
  • Comes in Black
 

 
Playstation 4 Pro

  • Released in 2016 making it the most current model of the PS4.
  • Supports 4K graphics and audio, allowing it to be the best console experience with the Playstation VR.
  • 1 TB or 2TB replacable hard Drive allows more storage for online purchases in our digital world.
  • Comes only in Black
  • Supports the same game library as the other models.
